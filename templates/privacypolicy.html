1. Introduction

Echelon Digital Solutions respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you interact with us through the WhatsApp Business API (provided by Meta).

By using our WhatsApp Business services, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1. Information You Provide

We may collect the following personal data when you communicate with us via WhatsApp:

  • Contact Information: Phone number, name, email address (if provided).
  • Message Content: Text, images, videos, documents, or other files shared in conversations.
  • Transaction Details: Order confirmations, payment details (if applicable).

2.2. Information Automatically Collected

  • Metadata: Timestamps, message status (delivered, read), and interaction logs.
  • Device Information: IP address, device type, operating system (collected via WhatsApp's API).

2.3. Information from Third Parties

If you interact with us through a linked Facebook/Instagram Business account, we may receive limited profile data (e.g., public name) as permitted by Meta's policies.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries.
  • Process orders, bookings, or service requests.
  • Send transactional updates (e.g., shipping confirmations).
  • Improve our services and personalize user experiences.
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud.

We do not use WhatsApp for telemedicine or to collect sensitive health/financial data unless explicitly consented and legally permitted.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

  • Meta (WhatsApp): As per their Business Terms of Service.

We retain your WhatsApp messages and data only as long as necessary for:
